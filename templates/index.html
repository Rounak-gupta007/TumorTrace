+ About Brain Tumor

A brain tumor is a mass or growth of abnormal cells in your brain. Some brain tumors are noncancerous (benign), and some brain tumors are cancerous (malignant). Brain tumors can begin in other parts of your body and spread to your brain as secondary (metastatic) brain tumors.
